关于夏威夷群岛 About the Hawaiian islands

夏威夷群岛位于太平洋几乎正中部，是波利尼西亚群岛中面积最大的一个二级群岛，该群岛呈弧状横贯北回归线，介于154°41′W-178°75′W、18°54′N~28°15′N之间，总面积16759km²。美国于1959年在此处设夏威夷州，州府在欧胡岛（Oahu）上的檀香山（Honolulu，火奴鲁鲁）。据檀香山毕夏普博物馆的资料，夏威夷最早居民的祖先可追溯至中国的长三角地区，时间大约在5000-6000年前。夏威夷群岛是世界著名的旅游胜地，其中的茂宜岛和可爱岛2013年世界排名第一、第二。 The Hawaiian islands are located almost in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, and are the largest second-level islands in Polynesia. The islands arc across the tropic of cancer, between 154°41 'w-178 °75' W and 18°54 'N~28°15' N, with a total area of 16759km squared.The United States established Hawaii here in 1959, and its capital is Honolulu on Oahu.According to the bishop museum in Honolulu, the earliest residents of Hawaii can be traced back to the Yangtze river delta in China, about 5,000 to 6,000 years ago.The Hawaiian islands are the world's most famous tourist destinations, with maui and kauai ranked first and second in 2013.

关于尼亚加拉瀑布 About Niagara Falls

尼亚加拉瀑布位于加拿大安大略省和美国纽约州的交界处，瀑布源头为尼亚加拉河，主瀑布位于加拿大境内，是瀑布的最佳观赏地；在美国境内瀑布由月亮岛隔开，观赏的是瀑布侧面。同时，该瀑布也是世界第一大跨国瀑布。尼亚加拉河的水流冲下悬崖至下游重新汇合，在不足2公里长的河段里以每小时35.4公里的速度跌荡而下15.8米的落差，演绎出世界上最狂野的漩涡急流，经过左岸加拿大的昆斯顿、右岸美国的利维斯顿，冲过“魔鬼洞急流”，沿着最后的“利维斯顿支流峡谷”由西向东进入安大略湖。 Niagara Falls is located at the border of Ontario, Canada and New York, the United States.Waterfalls in the United States are separated by moon island and viewed from the sides of the falls.It is also the world's largest transnational waterfall.The Niagara river downstream of the flow of water down the cliff to meet again, in less than 2 km long river under the precariousness at a speed of 35.4 kilometers per hour and 15.8 m drop, deduce one of the world's wild whirlpool rapids, passes through the left bank of Canada koons, the right bank of the United States the Galveston, through the "devil's hole jet", along the "Galveston tributary valley" from west to east into the lake Ontario.

关于塞班岛 About saipan

塞班岛（Saipan）是北马里亚纳群岛联邦首都。面积185平方公里，人口约52200人（2010年）。最高点塔波乔山（Tagpochau）海拔466米。现主要出产椰干，亦产芋、木薯、薯蓣、面包果及香蕉，设商船坞和国际机场。1944年被美军占领，成为美军重要的空军基地。1962年成为太平洋岛屿美国托管地的首府。2009年11月28日，美国联邦政府接管北马里亚纳群岛的移民事务。 Saipan is the federal capital of the northern mariana islands.It covers an area of 185 square kilometers and has a population of about 52,200 (2010).The highest point, Tagpochau, is 466 metres above sea level.The main products are dried coconut, yam, cassava, yam, bread fruit and banana.It was occupied by the us army in 1944 and became an important air base for the us army.1962 it became the capital of the United States trust territory in the Pacific islands.On November 28, 2009, the U.S. federal government took over immigration in the northern mariana islands.

北美洲国家的一些习俗 Some customs in north American countries
加拿大 Canadian

加拿大人热情好客。亲朋好友之间请吃饭一般在家里而不去餐馆，认为这样更友好。客人来到主人家，进餐时由女主人安排座位，或事先在每个座位前放好写有客人姓名的卡片。在加拿大还有一种请吃饭的方式更加随便，即“自助餐”或“冷餐会”形式。由主人把饭菜全部摆在桌上后，客人可各自拿一只大盘子（或由主人发给），自己动手盛取自己喜欢吃的食品，可以离开餐桌到另一房间随便就坐进餐，这样客人与主人，客人与客人之间便可有更多的时间交谈。在加拿大一般应邀去友人家里吃饭，不需送礼物。但如去亲朋家度周末或住几天，则应给女主人带点礼品，如一瓶酒、一盒糖等。离开主人家后，回到家中应立即给女主人写封信，告诉已平安抵家，并对受到的款待表示感谢。节假日访问亲友，通常也需要带一点礼物。 　　　　加拿大人不像美国人那样随便，大部分招待会在饭店和俱乐部举行。如果应邀去加拿大人家里做客，可以事先送去或随身携带上一束鲜花给女主人。但不要送白色的百合花，在加拿大，白色的百合花只有在葬礼上才用。拉着打开的门，让你后面的人先通过，这种礼仪会受人赞赏。 向过路人或电梯里遇到的陌生人微笑和说声“Hi”，表现友善的姿态。 Canadians are hospitable.Friends and relatives usually eat at home instead of going to restaurants, thinking it more friendly.When the guest comes to the host's house, the hostess will arrange the table, or put a card with the guest's name in front of each seat in advance.In Canada, there is a more casual way to invite people to dinner, which is in the form of "buffet" or "buffet dinner".After the host has put all the dishes on the table, each guest may take a large plate (or be given by the host) and help himself to the food he likes. He may leave the table and go to another room to have dinner at his own pleasure, so that there will be more time for conversation between the host and the guest.In Canada generally invited to a friend's home for dinner, do not need to send gifts.But if go to close friend home to spend a weekend or live a few days, answer to bring a bit gift to goodwife, wait like a bottle of wine, candy.When you return home, you should immediately write a letter to your hostess informing her of your safe arrival and thanking her for her hospitality.It is usually necessary to bring some gifts to visit friends and relatives during holidays. Canadians are not as casual as americans. Most receptions are held in restaurants and clubs.If invited to a Canadian's home, you can send a bouquet of flowers to your hostess or take it with you.But don't give white lilies. In Canada, white lilies are only used at funerals.The etiquette of holding an open door to let the person behind you pass first is appreciated. Smile and say "Hi" to a stranger passing by or in an elevator. 　

美国 America

　美国人不象英国人那样总要衣冠楚楚，而是不大讲究穿戴。但正式场合，美国人就比较讲究礼节了。　　　　接见时，要讲究服饰，注意整洁，穿着西装较好，特别是鞋要擦亮，手指甲要清洁。美国商人较少握手，即使是初次见面，也不一定非先握手不可，时常是点头微笑致意，礼貌地打招呼就行了。男士握女士的手要斯文，不可用力。如果女士无握手之意，男士不要主动伸手，除非女士主动。握手时不能用双手。上下级之间，上级先伸手握手。长幼之间，长者先伸手握手。主宾之间，主人先伸手。男性之间，最忌互相攀肩搭臂。美国人谈话时不喜欢双方离得太近，习惯于两人的身体保持一定的距离。一般应保持120-150厘米之间，最少也不得小于50厘米。应邀去美国人家中作客或参加宴会，最好给主人带上一些小礼品，如化妆品、儿童玩具、本国特产或烟酒之类。对家中的摆设,主人喜欢听赞赏的语言,而不愿听到询问价格的话。 Americans don't always dress as well as Englishmen. They don't pay much attention to clothes. But on formal occasions, Americans are more courteous. When interviewing, we should pay attention to clothing, pay attention to neatness, wear a suit better, especially shoes to polish, fingernails to clean. American businessmen rarely shake hands. Even when they meet for the first time, they do not necessarily have to shake hands first. They often nod, smile and greet politely. The man should hold the lady's hand gently, not forcefully. If a woman doesn't want to shake hands, don't reach out unless a woman does. Do not shake hands with your hands. Between superiors and subordinates, the superiors first extend their hands and shake hands. Between the elder and the younger, the elder first reaches out and shakes hands. Between the host and the guest, the host extends his hand first. Among men, shoulder-to-arm climbing is the most taboo. Americans don't like being too close to each other when talking. They are accustomed to keeping a certain distance between their bodies. Generally, it should be kept between 120 and 150 centimeters, at least not less than 50 centimeters. When invited to an American home or to a banquet, it is best to bring small gifts to the host, such as cosmetics, children's toys, local specialties or tobacco and alcohol. For home furnishings, the host likes to listen to the language of appreciation rather than ask for price. 　　　

巴拿马 Panama

巴拿马人交谈时喜欢双方的距离靠得近一些，他们认为这样才显得亲近自然，否则就有冷淡对方之嫌了，他们有一些手势语，如吻指尖表示赞美。　　　　在正式社交场合，他们同宾客见面时常行握手礼，而在亲朋好友之间，见面时常行拥抱礼，在街头遇见熟人时，则以点头为礼，当地人接电话，往往先问您是谁，这与欧美国家先自报家门的习惯不同。　　　　巴拿马人以大米和玉米为主食，他们制作的食品讲究五味俱全，比较有名的有大米，肉煮赤豆。巴拿马人很喜欢一种叫桑科乔的食品，当地人常以此宴请客人。哥斯达尼加 在哥斯达尼加，男子见面，一般行握手礼，关系非同一般的，还要拥抱。男子见到较熟悉的女子有的是轻吻她的右手，有的是吻脸。跟初次见面的人打交道，不能哈哈大笑，也不能询问对方的年龄种族，称呼时，可在名字后面加先生，女士。　　　　在哥斯达尼加，咖啡和香蕉产量极为丰富，其出口量占出口总量的一半，而且这两种东西成了他们日常生活中不可少的饮料和食品。 到当地人家里作客，他们一般都备有小吃或饮料招待，客人不应拒绝，否则会使主人感到难堪。墨西哥 墨西哥人通常的问候方式是微笑和握手，在亲朋好友之间也施亲吻礼和拥抱礼，但却忌讳不熟悉的男女之间互相亲吻。墨西哥人的姓名一般由教名，父姓，母姓三部分组成。在一般场合可用略称形式，即只用教名和父姓，妇女婚后改为夫姓，夫姓之前须加一个德字表示从属关系。 Panamanians like to be close to each other when they talk. They think this makes them seem close and natural, or else they might look cold to each other.In formal social occasions, they often shake hands with guests, while among relatives and friends, they often embrace each other. When they meet acquaintances in the street, they nod their heads. When the locals answer the phone, they often ask who you are first.Panamanians live on rice and corn. They make food with all kinds of flavor. Some famous foods are rice and red beans cooked with meat.Panamanians love a kind of food called sancho, which is often used to entertain guests.Costa rica In costa rica, men meet with a handshake, an unusual relationship, and a hug.When the man sees a familiar woman, he kisses her right hand or her face.When dealing with people you meet for the first time, you should not laugh out loud, and you should not ask about their age or ethnicity.In costa rica, coffee and bananas are so abundant that they account for half of all exports, and both are indispensable to their daily lives. When visiting local people, they are usually provided with snacks or drinks. Guests should not refuse, or it will embarrass the host.Mexico Mexicans usually greet each other with a smile and a handshake. They also give kisses and hugs to their relatives and friends, but it is taboo for unfamiliar men and women to kiss each other.Mexican names generally consist of Christian, paternal and maternal names.In general cases, the form of abbreviation can be used, that is, only the Christian name and the father's name, the woman changed to the husband's name after marriage, before the husband's name must add a German word to indicate the subordination. 　　　　　

古巴 Cuba

　两人相见，凡是似曾相识的，或者要与之办交涉的，总要打一个招呼。除了问候致意之外，还有一个称呼问题，男子称为先生，女子称小姐。在古巴人性格质朴，他们在社交场合与客人相见，一般习惯以握手为礼。在朋友或亲人相见时，也常以拥抱为礼，一般男人与男人之间，女人与女人之间都可拥抱。　　　　在古巴，人们大多信仰天主教，在饮食上基本以西餐为主。通常早餐是面包，牛油，午餐多吃三明治，牛奶，晚餐则多食肉类，蔬菜。 古巴人非常喜欢黄米饭，他们在饮食上最负盛名的菜肴是阿希亚科。这是用猪肉同君子兰，芋头，山药，古巴人一般不爱吃羊肉和海味品。古巴盛产水果，所以人们生活中离不开水果。他们把水果挤成汁，作为一种经常性的饮料。 there is a question of address, the man is called Sir, the woman is called miss.In Cuba, people have a simple personality. They meet their guests in social occasions and usually shake hands with them.When friends or relatives meet, also often to embrace for the ceremony, generally between men and men, women and women can hug.In Cuba, most people believe in Catholicism and mainly eat western food.Usually bread and butter for breakfast, sandwiches and milk for lunch and meat and vegetables for dinner. Cubans love yellow rice, and their most famous dish is acciaco.It's pork with clivia, taro, yams, and cubans generally don't like lamb or seafood.Cuba is rich in fruit, so people can't live without fruit.They squeeze fruit into juice as a regular drink.
