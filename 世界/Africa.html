关于胡夫金字塔 About the pyramid of khufu

胡夫金字塔是埃及金字塔之一。塔高146.59米，因年久风化，顶端剥落10米，现高136.5米，相当于40层大厦高。塔身是用230万块巨石堆砌而成，大小不等的石料重达1.5吨至160吨，塔的总重量约为684万吨，它的规模是埃及至今发现的70多座金字塔中较大的。它是一座几乎实心的巨石体，成群结队的人将这些大石块沿着金字塔内部的螺旋上升通道往上拖运，然后逐层堆砌而成。十万多个工匠共用约20年的时间才完成的人类奇迹。 The pyramid of khufu is one of the pyramids of Egypt.The tower is 146.59 meters high, due to weathering for a long time, the top peeling 10 meters, is now 136.5 meters high, equivalent to the height of a 40-story building.The tower is made of 2.3 million boulders, ranging in size from 1.5 tons to 160 tons. The total weight of the tower is about 6.84 million tons. It is the largest of more than 70 pyramids discovered in Egypt to date.It is an almost solid mass of boulders, which groups of people haul up and down spiraling channels inside the pyramid and pile up layer by layer.It took more than 100,000 craftsmen about 20 years to complete the human miracle.. 。

关于尼罗河 About the Nile

尼罗河是一条流经非洲东部与北部的河流，自南向北注入地中海，全长6670公里，是世界上最长的河流。与中非地区的刚果河以及西非地区的尼日尔河并列非洲最大的三个河流系统。2007年虽有来自巴西的学者宣称亚马逊河长度更胜一筹，但尚未获得全球地理学界的普遍认同。尼罗河有两条主要的支流，白尼罗河和青尼罗河。发源于埃塞俄比亚高原的青尼罗河是尼罗河下游大多数水和营养的来源，但是白尼罗河则是两条支流中最长的。 The Nile is a river flowing through east and north Africa, flowing from south to north into the Mediterranean Sea, the total length of 6670 kilometers, is the longest river in the world.Along with the Congo river in central Africa and the Niger river in west Africa, they are the three largest river systems in Africa.In 2007 a Brazilian academic claimed that the amazon was longer, but it was not widely accepted by the world's geographers.The Nile has two main tributaries, the white Nile and the blue Nile.Originating in the Ethiopian highlands, the blue Nile is the source of most water and nutrients in the lower reaches of the Nile, but the white Nile is the longest of the two tributaries.

关于维多利亚湖 About lake Victoria

维多利亚湖位于东非高原，大部分在坦桑尼亚和乌干达两国境内，一小部分属于肯尼亚。1860-1863年英国探险家约翰·汉宁·斯皮克和格兰特到此处调查尼罗河的源头时，以英国女王维多利亚的名字命名该湖泊。湖泊介于东非大裂谷及其西支之间，居裂谷间浅宽盆地的北部，湖盆是由于地面凹陷而形成的，所以维多利亚湖的成因与东非高原上的其它大湖是完全不同的。该湖的面积69400km²，是非洲最大湖泊，世界第二大淡水湖。湖面海拔1134米，鱼类资源丰富。 Lake Victoria is on the east African plateau, mostly in Tanzania and Uganda, and a small part in Kenya.The lake was named after British queen Victoria when British explorers John hanning speke and grant visited it in 1860-1863 to investigate the source of the Nile.The lake lies between the east African rift valley and its western branch, and lies in the north of the shallow and wide inter-rift basin. The lake basin is formed by the depression of the ground, so the genesis of lake Victoria is completely different from other great lakes on the east African plateau.With an area of 69400km ^ 2, it is the largest lake in Africa and the second largest fresh water lake in the world.Lake 1134 meters above sea level, rich in fish resources.
